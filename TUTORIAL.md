**What You Learn:** Introduction to the tutorial

**What You Learn:**

### Key Concepts to Remember

1. **Mana Management**
   - You get more mana each turn
   - Plan ahead for expensive cards
   - Don't waste mana

2. **Board Control**
   - Keep minions alive
   - Remove enemy threats
   - Control the battlefield

3. **Card Advantage**
   - More cards = more options
   - Don't overextend
   - Draw cards when possible

4. **Health Management**
   - Your health is a resource
   - Don't panic at low health
   - Win at 1 HP or 30 HP

5. **Timing**
   - When to attack
   - When to play cards
   - When to use hero power

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I skip the tutorial?
**A:** Yes! Press ESC or click "Skip" button anytime.

### Q: Can I replay the tutorial?
**A:** Yes! Just select "Tutorial" from the main menu again.

### Q: What if I don't understand something?
**A:** The tutorial repeats key concepts. You can also check:
- README.md for detailed rules
- QUICKSTART.md for quick reference
- FEATURES.md for complete feature list

### Q: Do I need to complete the tutorial?
**A:** No, but it's highly recommended for new players!

### Q: How long does the tutorial take?
**A:** About 5-10 minutes if you read everything carefully.

### Q: Can I pause the tutorial?
**A:** Yes, just press ESC to exit. Your progress won't be saved, but you can restart anytime.
